Board of directors

Volunteer oversight for mission and resources.

The board sets strategic direction, ensures fiscal responsibility, and advances JSSOF’s commitment to active-duty SOF members, their families, and veterans. For committee assignments or governance materials, contact mindy@jssof.org.

Rodger Slaughter

Slaughter served as an Army combat arms officer and Green Beret. He served in Vietnam, Desert Storm, and more over 30 years of military service. He continues to be active in the Special Forces community post-service. Slaughter co-founded and was president of the Special Forces Association of Montana and co-founded and is the current president of the Joint Service Special Operations Fund. Both are nonprofits committed to aiding military members and their families.

Patrick McCauley

CSM (R) Patrick McCauley is an accomplished Special Operations veteran who distinguished himself leading this nation’s most elite Special Operations organizations in diverse and challenging combat environments. Serving in the U.S. military for more than 33 years, he culminated his career as the ninth Command Senior Enlisted Leader for U.S. Special Operations Command. He is an Operating Partner at Data Venture Capital and a member of the Special Operations Warrior Foundation.

Andrew Graziano

Andrew Graziano is a finance and operations executive with experience in defense, aerospace, venture capital, and entrepreneurship. A former U.S. Army Special Operations finance leader, he now serves in CFO roles supporting emerging companies and investment platforms, is the founder of Firefly Truffles LLC, and serves as treasurer and board member for NDIA North Carolina. He brings boards strong financial oversight, strategic planning, and mission-driven leadership.

Gordon Magnus

Gordon served a 40+ year defense industrial base career with Texas Instruments, Raytheon, and RTX, developing and delivering warfighting systems and services for the Department of Defense and the intelligence community. He serves on the board of directors of Albers Aerospace and Delta Black Aerospace; is director of finance and board advisor for Choose to Soar Ministries; and serves RTX as a consultant for the defense industry.

Robert Beck

Bob is a former U.S. Army Ranger, jumpmaster, and Green Beret who served in Vietnam. He served as an officer in a large corporation prior to starting his own global specialty chemical company, Seaco Technologies Inc. He combines corporate management and entrepreneurial experience setting up company management and systems. Today he serves as president of Seaco Technologies Inc. and several other companies, including nonprofits. He enjoys flying his antique airplanes and collecting cars and motorcycles.

Respites

Respites designed for restoration and connection.

A Private Farm Retreat

A peaceful, bespoke retreat where exceptional farm-fresh food, warm fellowship, and thoughtful design come together.

Savor slow, delicious shared meals in curated spaces, and let the gentle rhythm of the farm invite deep connection, true relaxation, and quiet renewal.

Summer Respites

JSSOF offers a number of summer respites for our families as well as single active duty service members. Respites range from riding roller coasters, meeting your favorite cartoon characters, to serene fly fishing trips across the nation.

Winter Respite

Ranch Hands Reunion is our marquis event. JSSOF sponsors 72 Active-Duty Special Operations Soldiers and their families and Gold Star Family for a week of skiing, dog sledding, snowmobiling, hatchet throwing, feasting, and fire pits. This event is also attended by 60 to 80 Veterans who fund their own travel, lodging, meals, and activities.
